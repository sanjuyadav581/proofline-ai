4. cta_compliance — Does the CTA match approved formats for the channel?
5. channel_fit — Does the content match the channel's length, format, and structural requirements?
6. audience_fit — Is the tone and framing appropriate for the target audience?
7. tone_alignment — Does the content match the brand's voice guidelines (confident not arrogant, direct not blunt)?

Base your scores on the audit results, the brand rules, and the content itself.
Be calibrated: a score of 100 means perfect compliance. A score below 50 means significant issues.
